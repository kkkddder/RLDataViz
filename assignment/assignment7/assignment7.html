WeaBoard

WeaBoard displays current weather data by searching city names. Before entering city name, it will enclose info by differnt weather category icons. After a search, it will presents weather data into three parts.

Left upper: city name, sunrise time, sunset time, as well as a brief description of the weather.

Right upper: temperature, wind speed, humidity, and pressure. Moving mouse to unfold weather info.

Bottom: forecasting weather info in the coming five days, triggered by moving mouse on weather info categories from the right upper cells.

Have fun!!!
