Condegram Spiral Plot

Built FAR Per Lot by Major Years in Manhattan Neighborhood 01

I plan to show the detailed relationship between built year and built FAR per lot. Here I used a example of Manhattan Neighborhood 01 (MN01). Since the construction years of these lots range from 1758 to 2013, only a few major years were selected into this demo. This condegram spiral plot will be able to show the time change of built FAR in MN01. We could find that the built FAR in the early 20th century was much less than that later.

Compared to other horizontal or vertical long time serie charts, this plot has a square shape, which will make it easy to be fitted in websites or reports well. However, the lots within each built year were placed without order. If we could get the built date info, all the lots can be arranged based on dates.
